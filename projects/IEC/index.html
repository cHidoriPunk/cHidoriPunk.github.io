Question No.4

Cyber defense and cyber resilience policy (CDRP) is additional significant component of organizational cyber shield.

What, in your opinion are the unique characteristics of the cyber policy and why we differentiate between cyber defense and cyber resilience.

Place your Bets!

Times Up!

IEC ANSWER - Question No.4

  • Cyber policy deals with high level subjects and very detailed subject at the same time

  • Cyber policy changes in frequency that does not comply with the traditional policy behavior

  • Cyber policy is not defending tool – it is balancing tool

  • To defend is to build external and internal walls, to be resilient is to be able to recover when compromised, efficiently and with minimal damages.
